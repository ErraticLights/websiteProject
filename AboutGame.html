What is this Game?

Re-Action is a sidecrolling game where you play as John the Jumper to avoid enemies. As you run, you achieve a time representing the amount of time that you survived for, try to achieve a personal best or compete against others.

The game was created in Unity3D and contains content that was handmade by the developers. Everything that you see and hear is original content.
