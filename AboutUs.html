Who are we?

We are a small group of students from Patrician Brothers College who have an interest in the field of game production. We wanted to test out our skills and so we entered the Australian STEM Video Game Challenge. Together, with our Information and Software Technology teacher, we have worked on a sidescroller game. The game challenge submission date coincided with our in-class assignment of creating a website.
