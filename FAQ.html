What are the Controls and Goals of the Game?

Here is a video explanation:
The controls for the game are pretty simple. "W" to jump and "S" to slide. The game has no real goal, only to survive for as long as you can and compete against yourself or others for the best score.

What are the Enemies?

Within this game, there are two enemies throughout its entirety and they can not be killed. These enemies are the lizard and the ibis.
- The Lizard is a creature that crawls on the ground, it can be avoided by jumping.
- The Ibis is a flying creature, it can be avoided by sliding under it.
